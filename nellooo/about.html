About Us

Mbafon's Kitchen was founded by Nelson Mbafon, a passionate entrepreneur dedicated to providing durable and affordable kitchen utensils across Cameroon. With a vision to make cooking joyful and accessible, Mbafon leads a team of three loyal staff members: Ngong Esther, Tata Paul, and Ngang Linda.

As the CEO, Nelson oversees operations, product sourcing, and customer satisfaction. With a background in business management and a deep passion for culinary tools, he ensures every product meets the highest standards of quality and affordability. His leadership is centered on trust, teamwork, and innovation.

We operate from Monday to Saturday, between 8:00 AM and 6:00 PM, ensuring our customers get the best service and support throughout the week.
